Until the Opening of the Stratocumulus Gallery

The Stratocumulus Gallery makes history and art come alive in your home with AR technology. It is a new way to experience museums and interact with history. Stratocumulus allows you to engage with prehistoric animals, travel to remote islands, and delve more closely into the art pieces you desire.

Join us for the grand opening of the Stratocumulus Gallery! To start off the service with a bang, Stratocumulus will be hosting a guided tour to explore a series of art pieces created during the COVID-19 pandemic. Stratocumulus hopes to bridge the gap between museums and your home in order to make culture more accessible to everyone.

The audio for the pandemic tour will be made available after the opening for free so that all users will be able to enjoy it at their leisure.

Features

Features that Stratocumulus supports.

  • Users can walk through the museum and look at framed artwork
  • The user can bring up blurbs explaining each piece of art as well as links to more information
  • Audio tours

List of requirements

  • Oculus Quest 2 Headset
  • Fast internet connection
